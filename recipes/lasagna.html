Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 ¼ pounds ground turkey

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in ground turkey; cook until no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
    4. Drain excess grease.
